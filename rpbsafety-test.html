The RPB® Nova 3® is an advance on the RPB® Nova 2000™ in
comfort and functionality. It can be customized to suit individual
needs, further enhancing employees’ performance.

Tear-Off Lens System

The most reliable and intuitive system on the market

Individually pre-folded tabs ensuring workers are guaranteed to tear off only one lens at a time. Contractors have reported a time saving of 48 minutes per day, per blaster.

Streamlined
Air Flow System

Optical Design
