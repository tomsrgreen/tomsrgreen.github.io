MotionCards

What is a MotionCard?

How do you stand out in the sea of cards and emails that your clients and prospects receive every holiday? Sending a fully customized and branded MotionCard from Amplomedia is the professional and engaging way to show your clients that you’ve taken the time to make something really special for them. We’re able to make them as short or as long as you’d like in order to optimize your communication!

How can MotionCards be used?

> Email marketing campaigns
> Online CRM
> Client appreciation
> Christmas and other holidays

Themes

We are always working hard to develop MotionCard templates that will capture the excitement of all your favorite holidays–– and maybe a few you haven’t even thought about!
